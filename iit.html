About the International Institute of Technology (IIT)

North American Private University (Université nord-américaine privée) is a private university with an engineering school (International Institute of Technology – IIT), an architecture school, and a business school. Based in Sfax, Tunisia, it is accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research and ASIIN.

History

Established in 2012 by Dr. Chafik Abid, IIT aims to train future engineers, architects, and managers by providing robust national and international educational frameworks. The university has partnerships with global institutions, welcoming over 1200 students in 2022. Its new Sfax North campus opened in 2023.

Diplomas

  • International Institute of Technology (IIT): Offers preparatory and undergraduate tracks in Computer Engineering, Industrial Engineering, and more.
  • International School of Architecture (ISA): Provides undergraduate and graduate courses in Architecture.
  • International School of Business (ISB): Offers undergraduate and graduate programs in Business Administration.
